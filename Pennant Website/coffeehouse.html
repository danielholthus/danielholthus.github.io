Pennant Coffeehouse

Where the coffee flows endlessly

Or at least almost endlessly, anyway. We are a humble coffee shop started in 2013 in the small town McPherson, Kansas. Here at Pennant, we love coffee and we love people. We have a firm belief that coffee is something that can, and should, bring people together. Because of this, we have a deep love for coffee and everything that it stands for in our culture today: community, creativity, fellowship, etc.

These beliefs are what drive us to strive towards providing the best possible coffee and service that you can get. We hope you'll come share a cup with us.
