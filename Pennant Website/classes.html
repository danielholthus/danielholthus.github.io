Public Classes

Here at Pennant, we want everyone to be able to enjoy the craft of coffee just as much as we do. Which is why we offer a monthly class to the public the first Monday of every month at 6PM!

We teach on topics ranging from coffee extraction methods to milk steaming technique and latte art and anything in-between. We love talking about it, so we are always open to answering any questions anyone has and to having very open conversation at every class we teach! If you don't know where we are located, check out the 'Location + Hours' page.

Come hang out! We hope to see you there!
